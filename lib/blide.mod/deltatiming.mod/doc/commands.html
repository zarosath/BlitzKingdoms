Function SetMinimumTimeFactor(Factor:Double = DefaultMinimumTimeFactor)
DescriptionThis function sets the minimum time factor expected.
Informationso if the computer is too fast, the engine automatically adds the needed sleep calls, to avoid a too small delta factor, that could introduce some maths precision issues.
Usually a minimum of 0.5 millisec of time per frame is good for calculations. This has a pretended impact on FPS, making them more stable. After setting this, you should not be getting framerates superior to 100/Factor.
